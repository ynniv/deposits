Custodial Lightning wallets where the operator can't profitably steal. For every sat of user funds, they lock two of their own—one in direct reserves, one as collateral in other channels. Try to steal and partners slash your collateral. Every operation is signed and hash-chained, broadcast to multiple validators who catch any lie. If an operator cheats or disappears, time-locked recovery kicks in automatically. Users get the convenience of a custodial wallet with guarantees that don't depend on trusting the operator.
